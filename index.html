Sam Lummus

Welcome to my website that serves as a way for you to view what projects I have worked on and what I contributed to them. My name is Sam Lummus and I am going to Boise State University pursuing my bacholer's degree in GIMM (Game, Interactive Media, and Mobile). My native land is southern California, but I moved to Idaho after I graduated high school. I have been in Idaho for a couple years now, but I love it already. I have always been around technology and video games, as they have always interested me. I am a second year student currently and have delivered a couple of projects in my time here, which have taught me a lot about technical skills as well as important social skills when it comes to working in a group. I have worked with Unity game engine, C#, Java, and skills in Adobe Photoshop, Animate, and Illustrator. I am always eager to work on my next learning experience.

Golden Delight

This was my first attempt at a full fledged game from the ground up. I learned just how much effort and work goes into making a video game and makes me more appreciative of the ones I play myself. What was fun was how much creative freedom I had with the way the game went; I had total control over everything.
